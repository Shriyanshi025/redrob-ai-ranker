# Solution Approach

## Philosophy

The goal is not simply to match keywords but to identify candidates who are genuinely capable of succeeding in modern AI roles.

## Scoring Dimensions

### Skills
Measures alignment with AI, ML, NLP, LLM and Retrieval technologies.

### Experience
Rewards relevant industry experience while preventing excessive bias toward only senior candidates.

### Role Alignment
Understands similarity between candidate roles and target positions.

### Career Signals
Incorporates profile quality and professional activity.

## Why Hybrid Scoring?

Pure keyword search misses context.

Hybrid scoring combines:

- Skills
- Experience
- Role understanding
- Production readiness
- Career indicators

to create human-like ranking behaviour.
